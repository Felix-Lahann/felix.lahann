About Me

I'm Felix Lahann, a student at the University of Michigan studying Economics and Data Science. I'm drawn to data-driven problem-solving, human behavior, and organizational leadership — and I love exploring the intersections of psychology, economics, computer science, and philosophy to understand how people make decisions and how systems can better serve communities.
